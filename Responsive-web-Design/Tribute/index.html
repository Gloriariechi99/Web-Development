Matthew Langford Perry

Here is a timeline of Matthew Perry's Career

  • 1969 - Born in Massachusetts, United States
  • 1970 - First role as a child in 240-Robert
  • 1987 - Graduated from acting school
  • 1990 - First role as a child actor in 240-Robert
  • 1994 - Breakthrough with friends where he acted as chandler a fan favourite character which aired through 2004
  • 2022 - In first November his book Friends, Lovers and the Big Terrible Thing: A Memoir was released and he did a tour for the book. The book talks about his friends, love life and his addiction that he was able to overcome. Many people attribute his book to helping them also overcome his addiction.
  • 2023 - On October he was found dead in his bath tub after which might have been due to a heart attack
